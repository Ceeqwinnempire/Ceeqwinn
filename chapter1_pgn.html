📖 Chapter 1 (Pidgin): Sparkle Scatter

Na four times now I don dey run biro for those shiny squares like say dem go disappear if I pause — next thing? Wahala burst for corridor.

Chair waka, person shout “Omo!” even mop stick fall with style. Everywhere scatter.

Me sef shock small, turn look the drama, use hand wipe glitter from my holographic sleeve dress with crystal cuffs.

But I no let am shake me. Sharp sharp I reset back to the mission.

“Better times dey come!” Na the Director shout. Him voice be like preacher wey collect offering early.

I just give am one side-eye. Eye wey dey talk “wetin dey sup?”

“Thank God,” I yarn, push all the rainbow files give am like say na coded package.

“Give am glory,” he reply, hand come up slow like film. Golden card just dey shine for him hand like amebo wey no dey hide.
