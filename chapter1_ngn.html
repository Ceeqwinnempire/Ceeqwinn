📖 Chapter 1 (Naija English): Glitter Wahala

For the fourth time back-to-back, I dey scribble my initials into those shiny squares.

That’s when the hallway just scatter. Wahala!

I sharply turned toward the noise, my fingers casually brushing glitter off my holographic sleeve dress with crystal cuffs. But before my mind wander too far, I face my work again.

“Better days ahead,” Director thundered like he won election.

His eyes carry this weird combo of pride and tension.

“Thank God,” I muttered, pushing the colorful files to his end of the desk.

“Give Him glory,” he responded, stretching one hand toward me. In his palm? One golden card. E shine.
